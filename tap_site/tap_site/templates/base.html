Welcome to Wenyenchi

The Public Accountability Project (P.A.P)

This is a Citizen Engagement Platform whose sole intention is to mobilize a community of problem solvers.

Everyone has a voice! Tell your story!

Everyone has a right to be heard! Tell your story!

"SEMA USIKIKE"

The project consists of:-

Reviewer where community members get to rate public offices, instituitions and companies based on service delivery, diversity management, fiscal management, land, water & waste management, responsiveness to community grievances, healthcare, education e.t.c.

The reviewer writes a review and then rates their choice on a scale of 1 (worst) to 5 (best).

The community members will then use the information to generate reports and recommendations for each rated entity. The information will show the development progress of the country from the citizens' point of view.

Survey is geared at collecting huge amounts of data for a specific subject or area (ideal for companies, researchers and policy makers).

Polls is a place where community members can create and run their own specific polls. This is ideal for policy makers, pollsters and media professionals.

Wenyenchi has a recommendation system built on machine learning which connects you with other people who have reviewed a similar entity. In short it connects those who have shared problems and those who have similar experiences.
