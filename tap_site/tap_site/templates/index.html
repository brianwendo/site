Welcome to Wenye-nchi - The Public Accountability Project (PAP)

This is a Kenyan crowdsourcing platform whose sole intention is to create a community of problem solvers.

If man can't learn how to solve life's problems, mankind is doomed.

The project consists of a number of apps each providing a solution to a particular problem.

Reviewer App where community members get to rate public offices, instituitions and companies based on service delivery, diversity management, fiscal management, land, water & waste management, responsiveness to community grievances, healthcare, education e.t.c.

The reviewer writes a review and then rates their choice on a scale of 1 (worst) to 5 (best). The community members will then use the information to generate reports and recommendations for each rated entity. The information will show the development progress of the country from the citizens' point of view.

Survey App is geared at collecting huge amounts of data for a specific subject or area (ideal for companies, researchers and policy makers).

Polls App is a place where community members can create and run their own specific polls. This is ideal for individuals, pollsters and media professionals.

Petitions App is a place where petitioners can create and sign petitions to spur action for a cause they are championing.

Music App is a place where artists, deejays, spoken word artists and podcasters upload and share their creations. The artists, deejays, spoken word artists and podcasters will be added as creators and their events will be featured on the

Events App;which is a catalogue of Kenya related events happening across the world including all exhibitions, workshops, trainings, book launches, readings, concerts, festivals, auditions, films, plays and all cinema schedules complete with cast, crew and all featured performers.

Classifieds App is a place where community members and entrepreneurs can post ads for jobs, products and services.

Message App is a place where community members can interact and engage with each other including staff.

Helpdesk App is a place where community members can raise issues with the administrator and staff members.

News App is where community members share and publish their stories.
